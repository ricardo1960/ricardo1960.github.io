Sea \(I\) = Estudiar inglés y \(A\) = Estudiar alemán

\begin{equation*} P(I)=0.78, P(A)=0.23 \text{y} P(I\cap A)=0.15 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} P(I \cup A)=P(I)+P(A)-P(I \cap A)=0.78+0.23-0.15=0.86 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} P(\overline{I} \cap \overline{A})=P( \overline{ I \cup A})=1-P(I \cup A)=1-0.86=0.14 \end{equation*}
